Background

The coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic is the defining global health crisis of our time. Since its emergence in Asia late last year, the virus has spread to spread to every continent except Antarctica. Cases are on the rise all over the world.

Countries all over the world are trying to limit the spread of this respiratory disease. Their efforts include testing and treating patients, carrying out contact tracing, quarantining citizens, limiting travel, and cancellation of large gatherings.

COVID-19 is not merely a health crisis. This pandemic has the potential to leave deep scars by creating devastating political, economic, and social crises.

Our communities are now unrecognizable. The once crowded and busy cities lay bare as its residents stay indoors, either by government order or personal choice.
